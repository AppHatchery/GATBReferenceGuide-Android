About the Developer

The AppHatchery is a collaboration between Emory University and Georgia Tech as part of the Georgia Clinical and Traslational Science Alliance (Georgia CTSA)



About the App

This app is a digital translation of the Georgia TB Reference guide, a booklet that responds to clinicians’ questions about tuberculosis infection, disease, and control. The standards and guidelines are based on the work and experience of the American Thoracic Society (ATS), the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), the Infectious Disease Society of America (IDSA), Emory University, the World Health Organization (WHO), and the Atlanta TB Prevention Coalition.
